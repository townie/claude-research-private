**Your TikTok content is NOT your profit center.**

Videos exist for one purpose: pushing eyeballs into your funnel. The actual money is made after the click. This document covers how to architect a funnel that maximizes revenue from every visitor.

```
- CTR = Click-Through Rate to your funnel
- CVR = Conversion Rate (visitors who buy)
- AOV = Average Order Value (total per customer)
```

**Key insight:** Improving any variable multiplies total revenue. A 2x improvement in AOV = 2x revenue.

## Front-End Offer Design

### Purpose
- Acquire the customer at break-even or small loss
- Build trust and relationship
- Identify buyers from browsers
- Set up for backend monetization

### Characteristics of Winning Front-Ends

1. **Low-friction price point** - $7, $17, $27, $37
2. **Immediate value delivery** - Digital products work best
3. **Quick win included** - Customer sees result fast
4. **Natural upsell path** - Logically leads to OTOs
5. **High perceived value** - Worth 10x the price

### Front-End Product Types

**Digital Products:**
- Ebooks/guides
- Video courses (mini)
- Templates/swipe files
- Software tools
- Checklists/frameworks

**Example by Niche:**

| Fitness | "30-Day Shred Meal Plan" | $19 |
| Dating | "First Message Templates Pack" | $17 |
| Productivity | "The Focus Framework Kit" | $27 |

## Order Bump Design

### Purpose
- Increase AOV at point of highest intent
- Push from loss/break-even into profit
- No additional friction (one checkbox)

### Order Bump Formula

**Must be:**
- Directly related to front-end
- Instantly deliverable
- Priced 30-50% of front-end
- One-sentence explainable

### Order Bump Examples

## One-Time Offer (OTO) Strategy

### OTO 1: The Accelerator

**Purpose:** Help customer get result FASTER

**Positioning:**
- "You're clearly serious about [result]..."
- "For people who want results in days, not months..."
- "The shortcut to [desired outcome]..."

**Price:** 3-7x the front-end ($97-197)

**Take rate target:** 10-20%

**Product types:**
- Done-for-you version
- Advanced training
- Software/automation tool
- Community access
- 1-on-1 coaching call

### OTO 2: The Expander

**Purpose:** Complement and expand what they've bought

**Positioning:**
- "Now that you have [OTO1], you need..."
- "The missing piece most people overlook..."
- "Complete your toolkit with..."

**Price:** Slightly less than OTO1 ($47-97)

**Take rate target:** 8-15%

**Product types:**
- Additional training modules
- Different use cases
- Advanced templates
- Bonus resource bundle
- Extended support

## Checkout Optimization

### Reducing Cart Abandonment

1. **Minimize form fields** - Name, email, payment only
2. **Progress indicators** - Show where they are
3. **Trust badges** - SSL, payment logos, guarantees
4. **Exit-intent popup** - "Wait! Here's 10% off"
5. **Abandoned cart email** - Recover within 24 hours

## Email Capture & Follow-Up

### Lead Magnet Strategy

Capture emails BEFORE the sale to:
- Retarget non-buyers
- Build long-term relationship
- Reduce dependency on TikTok

**Lead Magnet Types:**
- Free guide/checklist
- Mini-course (3-5 emails)
- Quiz/assessment
- Template/swipe file
- Webinar registration

### Email Sequence (Post-Purchase)

**Day 0:** Welcome + delivery
**Day 1:** Quick win / getting started
**Day 2:** Success story + FAQ
**Day 3:** OTO reminder (if not purchased)
**Day 7:** Check-in + testimonial request
**Day 14:** Complementary offer
**Day 30:** Renewal/continuation offer

### Email Sequence (Non-Buyer)

**Day 0:** Lead magnet delivery
**Day 1:** Value email + soft pitch
**Day 2:** Objection handling
**Day 3:** Social proof
**Day 5:** Scarcity/urgency
**Day 7:** Final offer
**Day 14+:** Nurture sequence

## AOV Optimization Tactics

### Quick Wins

1. **Add order bump** - +20-40% to AOV
2. **Add OTO 1** - +30-50% to AOV
3. **Test price points** - Often can increase 20%+
4. **Improve bump copy** - +10-15% bump take rate
5. **Video on OTO page** - +15-25% OTO conversions

### Advanced Tactics

1. **Tripwire offers** - Super low front-end ($1-7)
2. **Payment plans** - Increase OTO conversions 30%+
3. **Subscription offer** - Recurring revenue
4. **High-ticket backend** - $500-5000 coaching/done-for-you
5. **Cross-sell sequences** - Email to other products

## Common Funnel Mistakes

1. **No order bump** - Leaving easy money on table
2. **Weak OTO positioning** - Must feel essential
3. **Too many options** - Causes decision paralysis
4. **No email capture** - Missing retargeting opportunity
5. **Slow page load** - Kills conversions
6. **Mobile not optimized** - 70%+ traffic is mobile
7. **No urgency/scarcity** - Missing conversion lever
8. **Poor guarantee** - Increases buyer resistance
