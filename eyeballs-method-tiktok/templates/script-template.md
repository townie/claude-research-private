It was completely different because {reason}.

## Hook Bank

### Curiosity Hooks
- "Nobody is talking about this..."
- "I wasn't supposed to share this, but..."
- "The {industry} doesn't want you to know this..."
- "Here's what {successful people} do differently..."
- "Stop doing {common thing} immediately..."

### Result Hooks
- "I made ${amount} doing this..."
- "This changed everything for me..."
- "How I went from {before} to {after}..."
- "The exact method I used to {result}..."

### Pattern Interrupt Hooks
- "Stop scrolling if you want to {benefit}..."
- "You need to hear this..."
- "This is the only {thing} you need..."
- "Forget everything you know about {topic}..."

### Authority Hooks
- "After {X years} in {industry}..."
- "{Number} mistakes I made so you don't have to..."
- "What I wish I knew {time} ago..."
- "As someone who {credential}..."

### Controversial Hooks
- "Unpopular opinion: {statement}"
- "This is going to upset some people..."
- "{Popular thing} is overrated. Here's why..."
- "I don't care what anyone says, {opinion}..."
